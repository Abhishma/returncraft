# Policy v1

## damage_or_defect
Claims for damaged or defective items may be approved if submitted within 7 days of delivery and supported by clear photo evidence.

## wrong_item
Claims for wrong-item delivery may be approved if submitted within 7 days and order mismatch is verified.

## discounted_item_exclusion
Discounted items are not eligible for return or exchange unless damaged, defective, or wrong item was delivered.

## international_return_exclusion
International orders are not eligible for return or exchange unless damage or wrong item is confirmed.

## evidence_required
Photos or equivalent evidence are required for damage claims.

## manager_discretion
Borderline cases may be escalated for manual goodwill review.
